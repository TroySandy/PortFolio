About Me

'The sea, once it casts its spell, holds one in its net of wonders forever.' -Jacques Cousteau

Hello there! You probably are wondering why this clearly aquatic person showed up on your search for a WebDev? Well, my previous career and educational path started in the Underwater Science and Archeology field at Indiana University. Completely in love with the ocean, I devoted all my time and energy into scuba diving and seeing all the things underwater! But as time passed, I realized I was looking for more out of life than that. So I traded my dive computer and wetsuit for a laptop and tablet. Diving head-first into the world of code, I bring my unique experiences as a explorer, leader, instructor, and team member to the table.

Since enrolling in the 12-week coding bootcamp at Eleven Fifty, I have learned how to make beautiful and functional websites and programs. Below is an example of my work since starting the course. I am passionate about providing an easy and seamless way for the world to all the information the web has to offer.
